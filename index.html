ABOUT US

Studio Anthropology & Co. designs physical products, CMF systems, and packaging for companies whose ambition has outgrown their design. We work at the intersection of human behaviour, material intelligence, and Indian use contexts.

Our work spans medical hardware, consumer products, packaging systems, and social innovation; from cerebral oximeters for Indian clinical settings in collaboration with Tata Elxsi, to inclusive design systems for marginalised communities.

Rooted in culture. Built for the future.
