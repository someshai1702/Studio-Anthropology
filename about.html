About SAC

We design physical things. Products you can hold, systems you can feel, experiences that make sense the moment you touch them.

Studio Anthropology & Co. is a Pune-based multidisciplinary design studio working at the intersection of industrial design, human behaviour, and material intelligence. From medical hardware to beauty tech, from defence concepts to inclusive systems; we design for the full range of human experience.

Our Values

Human-Centered

Every design begins with understanding real human needs, behaviors, and aspirations.

Material Intelligence

We respect materials and let their inherent properties guide our design decisions.

Systemic Thinking

Looking beyond the product to understand the entire ecosystem it exists in.
